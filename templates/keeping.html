Where in the World Have I Lived?

March 28th, 1995-February 1996

Los Angeles, California

I was born in Los Angeles California on March 28th, 1995. My parents and I lived there until just before my first birthday.

February 1996-December 2003

Mendham, New Jersey

My parents moved to Mendham, New Jersey, and had my two sisters. We lived in Mendham until the end of 2003, when I was 8 years old and in third grade.

December 2003-June 2006

Irvine, California

I finished third grade in Irvine, California. This was my first big move and I struggled to fit in. We lived there until the end of 5th grade in 2006 when I was 11.

June 2006-June 2008

Brentwood, Tennessee

I started middle school in Brentwood, Tennessee. I was a fish out of water in the south, but I adapted and made some of the greatest friends I've ever had. We left after 7th grade in 2008, and I was 13.

June 2008-Present

Mendham, New Jersey

I finished middle school back in Mendham, New Jersey. Even though I had lived here already, and for so long, this was the hardest move I experienced. I expected everything to be as it was before, but I was wrong and had to make all new friends. We still live in Mendham and I am grateful that I got to graduate Kindergarten and High School with the same people.

August 2013-Present

Ann Arbor, Michigan

Going to college in Michigan was an expected surprise, and I wouldnt have it any other way. You can read more about my journey to University of Michigan and the School of Information here

Coming Soon: January 2016

London, England

Moving around has taught me to push myself outside of my comfort zone, and next semester I plan to do just that. I will be studying Management and Computer Science at University College, London. I can't wait to meet new people and live in the city I have dreamed about since I was 15!
